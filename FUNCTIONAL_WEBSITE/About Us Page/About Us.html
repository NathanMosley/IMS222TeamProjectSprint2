About Us

Our Group and Our Purpose

We are a group of Miami U. students advancing our knowledge of web design and development to push towards and achieve our individual degrees! We made this as a project for our coding class, and we wanted a website for video game reviews and all the like. Because like you, we too love video games and want to share our passion with you! It has been a super fun experience to learn coding, and there are so many different ways to express different attributes throughout the website you build.
